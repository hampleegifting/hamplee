Kashmiri Kahwa Tea

Dry Leaf : Beautiful, colourful blend of medium sized green tea leaves with various Indian spices.
Infusion : Bright-green leaves, with colourful spice bits
Strain and ready to enjoy : Bright-yellow, spicy with mellow notes. Vegetal, with a slightly sweet and spicy touch.

A popular breakfast tea among the Kashmiris, this exotic blend is made from fine green tea leaves and spices like almonds, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves and rose petals. With a spicy flavour and wonderful aroma, it leaves your mind refreshed.
